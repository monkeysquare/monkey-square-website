A Little Bit About Us

What is Monkey Square

Monkey Square is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization with no hidden agenda to evangelize cross platform and open-source development in .NET. Monkey Square is the parent organization of the annual MonkeySpace Conference and is a visible presence and point of contact for corporate sponsors interested in supporting the conference and other open-source .NET activities.

Our goal is to bring some structure to a fragmented ecosystem by helping to fund open-source .NET development and by funding special projects. Only together can we change the culture around our ecosystem.
